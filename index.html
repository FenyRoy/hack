About the event

.hack() is an event that devices to bring students from across the country to compete and bring out solutions for problems they are surrounded by.
The event aims at creating opportunities that students generally lack, real-life working experience, rapid problem-solving skills, thinking in terms of product, engaging in a team event in order to support and grow and finally for the ultimate exposure to the industry.

To devise viable ideas to solve real-life problems we see,to set up a platform for the application of the software stacks learned by the participants. And last, to get along with industry professionals and gaining the experience they share.
All in all the vision of the coordinators would be to mold a new generation of engineers competent and well informed in this era of rapid technological advancements.
